## Idea A — "Warm Authority" (Chosen)

**Design Movement:** Modern Humanist — warm professionalism meets accessible clarity.

**Core Principles:**
1. Trust through warmth — navy blue anchors authority, gold signals value and optimism
2. Generous whitespace — breathing room reduces cognitive load for the audience
3. Large, legible typography — accessibility-first, never sacrificing readability
4. Card-based content architecture — digestible, scannable, never overwhelming

**Color Philosophy:**
- Navy Blue (#1B3A6B) — authority, stability, trustworthiness
- Warm Gold (#C9922A) — achievement, energy, optimism
- Soft Cream (#FDF8F0) — warmth, approachability, not clinical white
- Charcoal (#2D2D2D) — readable body text
- Light Sage accent (#E8F0E9) — calm, supportive section backgrounds

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric card grid with a sticky bottom nav bar. Hero section uses a bold left-aligned headline with a warm gradient background. Sections feel like chapters in a guidebook — structured but human.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gold left-border accent on tip cards (like a bookmark)
2. Circular avatar/icon badges in navy with gold outlines
3. Subtle paper-texture background on hero section

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Accordion-style expand/collapse for interview scripts. Checklist items animate with a satisfying checkmark. Cover letter preview fades in gracefully.

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up on scroll for cards (staggered 100ms delay)
- Checkboxes: scale + color transition on check
- Bottom nav: active tab indicator slides smoothly
- Cover letter generation: fade-in with slight scale

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — dignified, authoritative, warm
- Body: Source Sans Pro (sans-serif) — clean, highly readable at 16px+
- Hierarchy: 32px display / 22px section headers / 18px card titles / 16px body

## Idea B — "Bold Clarity"

**Design Movement:** Swiss Grid Modernism — structured, high-contrast, no-nonsense

**Core Principles:**
1. Grid discipline — everything aligned to an 8px baseline grid
2. High contrast — black on white, gold on navy only
3. Typography as design — headlines do the heavy visual lifting
4. Zero decoration — every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:** Black, white, electric gold (#F5A623). Stark but energetic.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width horizontal bands alternating white/navy. No cards — just bold type blocks.

**Signature Elements:** Oversized section numbers, thick gold rule dividers, uppercase labels

**Typography System:** Bebas Neue display / IBM Plex Sans body

## Selected Approach: **Idea A — "Warm Authority"**

This design philosophy best serves Janice's audience: adults 50+ who need to feel respected, empowered, and guided — not patronized or overwhelmed. The navy/gold/cream palette communicates professional credibility while the warm tones signal that this is a human, supportive tool. Playfair Display gives the app a dignified, trustworthy feel that differentiates it from generic job apps.
